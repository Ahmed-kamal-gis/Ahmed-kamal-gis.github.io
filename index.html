// About Me

Engineering Precision
Through Spatial Data

I'm a GIS Engineer based in the Eastern Province of Saudi Arabia, with over 3 years of hands-on experience delivering geospatial solutions for utility infrastructure, urban planning, and environmental analysis.

My work centers on transforming complex field data — from survey measurements to geotechnical records — into clean, validated, submission-ready GIS datasets that meet the exact standards of clients like the National Water Company.

Beyond data processing, I develop custom Python and ArcPy tools that automate repetitive workflows, and I bridge the gap between GIS and BIM to support modern infrastructure project delivery.
